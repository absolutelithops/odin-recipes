Peanut Butter Blossoms

Description

These delicious cookies only require a few simple ingredients and are so fun to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar, divided
  • 1 (18oz) jar smooth peanut butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 36 Hershey's Kisses, unwrapped

Steps

  1. Preheat the over to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Please 1/2 cup sugar into a shallow bowl.
  2. Stir peanut butter, remaining 1 1/4 cups sugar, and eggs together in a mixing bowl until well combined. Roll into 36 small balls.
  3. Roll balls in reserved sugar until coated, then arrange 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated over until very lightly browned on the bottom and the edges are beginning to crack, 12 to 14 minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven and immediately press one chocolate kiss into the center of each cookie. Cool on the baking sheets for 1 to 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely, about 15 minutes.
